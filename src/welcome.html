How to use

1

Find the extension icon

Look for the red blocked-play icon in your Chrome toolbar (top-right). Don't see it? Click the 🧩 puzzle piece and pin Shorts Blocker.

2

Click the icon to open the panel

A small panel appears. It's ON by default — Shorts are already blocked the moment you install.

3

Toggle Shorts on or off

Flip the switch anytime. Changes apply instantly — no page refresh needed.

4

Browse YouTube normally

Visit youtube.com as usual. The extension runs silently, hiding Shorts on every page automatically.

Common questions

Do I need to refresh YouTube after toggling? ▼
No. The extension injects a live CSS rule directly into the page — toggle it and Shorts vanish (or reappear) immediately.
Will my setting be remembered? ▼
Yes. Your preference is saved in Chrome's sync storage, so it persists across sessions and even syncs between devices if you're signed into Chrome.
Does this affect regular YouTube videos? ▼
Not at all. Only Shorts-specific elements are targeted. Regular videos, playlists, channels, and all other content are completely untouched.
What if some Shorts still slip through? ▼
YouTube sometimes updates its page structure. Try toggling the extension off and back on, or reload the page. The extension re-applies its rules automatically as you navigate.
